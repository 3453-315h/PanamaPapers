Intangibles: Non-physical assets that includes patents, trademarks, goodwill etc. Only those acquired from other companies are disclosed.
Net tangible assets: The actual book value of a company less liabilities etc.
Unremitted foreign earnings: Money that has not been disclosed to avoid triggering tax activity. The earnings may already be 'onshore' in the US.
Tax avoided: Taxes owed against 35% US federal tax rate from unremitted earnings.
Offshore chash: The dollar value of unremitted foreign earnings that is held offshore in cash form.
